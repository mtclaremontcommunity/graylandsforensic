Who we are

Residents, reading the public record

This site is maintained by the Mount Claremont Community Network: local parents and residents who read the publicly available documents about this project and set out what they show, with a source for every point.

Where we stand

We support forensic mental health services being properly resourced in Western Australia. Western Australia needs them. Our question is whether this site, this process, and this standard of evidence are adequate — not whether forensic patients deserve care, and not a partisan position on any side of politics.

Every concern on this site is drawn from a government document, a parliamentary record, or credible mainstream media — never from speculation, and never from social media or unverified accounts. Where we say a record doesn't exist, that reflects a thorough review of everything publicly available as at July 2026. If such a record exists and simply hasn't been released, that absence is itself a transparency concern worth answering.

We are not seeking to relitigate whether forensic mental health facilities should exist. We're asking that a $698 million decision affecting a residential suburb and four schools be made the way major infrastructure decisions are supposed to be made — with evidence, consultation, and a public explanation for what changed and why.
