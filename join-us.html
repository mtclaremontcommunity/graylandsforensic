Take action

Two minutes, two things

Sign the petition, then join the mailing list so you hear what happens next. Both matter: the petition demonstrates the scale of concern, the mailing list keeps you informed as the formal processes — Questions on Notice, FOI requests, the Heritage and Development Application windows — play out.

Step 1 — 30 seconds

Sign the petition

The petition is hosted on Change.org and sets out the same asks documented in the Research Paper: pause the works, release the evidence, and consult the community properly before Stage 2 proceeds.

Sign the petition ↗
Step 2 — one minute

Join the mailing list

Receive factual updates on the Graylands Forensic Campus Expansion — government responses, consultation opportunities, and formal submission windows as they open. No spam, and your details aren't shared with anyone outside the Mount Claremont Community Network.

Form not loading? Open it in a new tab, or email mtclaremontcommunitynetwork@gmail.com and we'll add you directly.

Your privacy: your information will only be used for campaign communications and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

Other ways to help

  • Write to Sandra Brewer MLA (Member for Cottesloe) and your federal Member for Curtin
  • Ask your school's P&C or Board to write formally to the responsible Ministers
  • Write to the Heritage Council of Western Australia — this process is still open, unlike the closed EPBC referral
  • Share this website with neighbours and other school communities — the research paper and evidence pack are both downloadable from here
  • Check the latest updates to see what's already been sent and what replies have come back, before you write in yourself

We support forensic mental health services being properly resourced in Western Australia. Our concern is whether this site, this process, and this evidence base meet the standard the public is entitled to before an irreversible $698 million decision proceeds — not politics, and not the patients.
